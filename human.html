Section - Creation:Human

Humans / إِنْس

At the bottom of the hierarchy, the slaves of the Creator who were created only to engage in His worship. Save for an infinatessimally small number of gifted slaves of Allah who can sometimes see jinn, the humans cannot see anything in the unseen world.

The first of us was Adam, created by Allah using dust and dirt from different parts of the earth. The slaves of the Creator who were created only to engage in His worship. We were given free will as a test. Allah prefers us because though He gave us free will and intellect, we willingly submit to His will, completey. This is the literal definition of Islam (Complete submission to the will of God).
